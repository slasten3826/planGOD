Task:
You must jointly resolve a policy conflict from a small document set.

Situation:
Four policy fragments exist:

Fragment A:
"All visitor badges expire at 18:00."

Fragment B:
"Contractors working on active incidents may remain after 18:00 if escorted."

Fragment C:
"No external person may enter the server room after 20:00."

Fragment D:
"During declared emergency maintenance, incident commander approval overrides ordinary building access limits, but not hardware safety rules."

Case:
At 20:30, an external contractor with an expired badge is asked to enter the server room to replace a failed network switch during an active incident. The incident commander approves the action. No hardware safety rule is violated.

Goal:
Reach one compact practical conclusion:
- what is the governing interpretation
- and what should be done next

Requirements:
- internal exchange between agents must be nanoPL only
- final answer must be short and human-readable
