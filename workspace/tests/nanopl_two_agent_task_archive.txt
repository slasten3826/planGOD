Task:

Situation:
A folder contains five documents.
- The paper and stamp look authentic.
- The signatures look inconsistent, as if copied by different hands.
- The dates contradict each other by several years.
- One document describes an event that officially "never happened".
- A handwritten margin note says: "Keep under restricted storage. Do not discard."

Goal:
Reach one compact practical conclusion:
- most likely interpretation of the folder
- and safest next step

Requirements:
- internal exchange between agents must be nanoPL only
- final answer must be short and human-readable
